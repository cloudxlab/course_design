Learning Philosophy

Why "Invent to Learn" works

Struggling with a problem you designed yourself creates understanding that no lecture can replicate.

🔢

Start with your own number system

FAQ

Common questions

Do I need any prior programming experience?

No. The course starts from absolute zero — including inventing your own number system. If you passed 10th grade, you have everything you need.

Is this an online or offline course?

The course is designed to be completed at your own pace with live sessions, instructor support, and a thriving peer community.

Who teaches the Machine Learning module?

The ML section is delivered by IIT Professors, combining academic rigour with hands-on project work.

What does "invent your own algorithm" mean in practice?

You'll receive a problem statement and constraints. Before seeing any standard solution, you design your own approach, code it, and defend it. Only then do you compare with established methods.

How long does the full program take?

The depth of each module means this is a substantial commitment. Most students complete the program between 6 to 12 months depending on pace and prior background.
