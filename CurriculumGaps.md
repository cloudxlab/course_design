## 5. Structural Suggestions

**18. Alumni Mentorship**
Pair each new cohort student with an alumnus from a prior batch. The cost is zero; the value for motivation and career guidance is high.

**19. Mock Interview Sessions**
Given the explicit goal of cracking SDE/ML/DS interviews, 2–3 dedicated mock interview sessions (algorithm round + ML breadth round) near the end of the program would directly serve the job-readiness promise.

**20. Explicit Ethics / Responsible AI module**
One session on bias in training data, fairness, and model interpretability (SHAP). This is increasingly a job interview topic and the right thing to include in a course that produces people building AI systems.
